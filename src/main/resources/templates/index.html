MINESWEEPER

Choose Difficulty Level:

Beginner (8x8, 10 mines) Expert (16x16, 40 mines)

How to Play:

  • Left-click on a square to reveal what's underneath
  • Right-click to place a flag where you think a mine is
  • Numbers indicate how many mines are in the adjacent squares
  • Clear all squares without mines to win
  • If you hit a mine, game over!
